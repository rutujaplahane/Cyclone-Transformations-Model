The data for cyclone driven changes in ocean thermodynamics was taken from Copernicus Marine Services (CMEMS-ARMOR3D).
The data was downloaded for 15 days before the cyclone formation and 15 days after the cyclone formation and the study area is Chilika coastal ocean  and can be seen in 
The data is a 4 dimensional data with the coordinates - time, latitude, longitude and depth. 
The oceanographic variables are temperature, salinity, geostrophic zonal velocity, geostrophic meridional velocity and absolute height.
